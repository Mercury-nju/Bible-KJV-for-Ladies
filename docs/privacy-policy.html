Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to KJV Bible ("Bible – KJV for Ladies"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally:

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Reading progress and bookmarks
  • Highlights and notes
  • App preferences and settings

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not collect usage analytics
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Photo Library Access

Our app requests access to your photo library solely for the purpose of:

  • Saving verse cards you create to your photo library
  • Adding images to your personal notes

We do not upload, share, or access your photos for any other purpose.

Data Storage

All data created within the app (bookmarks, highlights, notes) is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure data storage. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All your app data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
